Purpose

Why this platform exists

The goal is to make SEE study organized, simple, and less confusing.

🎯

Clear learning path

Students can choose a medium, open a subject, select a chapter, and start studying without confusion.

🧠

Revision friendly

Notes, MCQs, mock tests, important questions, and past questions are placed around each chapter.

⚡

Fast and simple

The website is static, lightweight, and easy to run using only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

For students

Built for SEE Grade 10 learners

see2083 is designed for students who want a clean place to study subjects, chapters, practice questions, and revise before exams.

  • English Medium students
  • Nepali Medium students

  • Teachers and content contributors
