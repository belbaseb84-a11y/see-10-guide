Purpose

Why this platform exists

see2083 helps SEE Grade 10 students study chapter by chapter. Students can choose English Medium, Nepali Medium, or Electrical Engineering, then open subjects, chapters, notes sections, MCQ practice, mock tests, past questions, search, and bookmarks in one clean place.

🎯

Clear learning path

Students can choose a medium, open a subject, select a chapter, and start studying without confusion.

🧠

Revision friendly

Notes, MCQs, mock tests, important questions, and past questions are placed around each chapter.

⚡

Fast and simple

The website is static, lightweight, and easy to run using only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
